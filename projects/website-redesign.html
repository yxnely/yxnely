Description:

Security First Insurance, a homeowners insurance company, needed a website redesign for consumers to learn about the company and product offerings.

About the Project

The UX team, along with Marketing team input, worked to define the brand guidelines, tone, and brand position using personas, mood boards, and war room sessions. The task was to reimagine the brand and position it atop of its competitors by promoting the products effectively, using testimonials, and an easy navigational layout to name a few.


I played a big role in the user research for personas, working with stakeholders to help define the brand, and defining the brand UI components.
