Playing Cards

Statement

Playing cards are so fun to make because you have just the right amount of restriction and freedom where you can let out and show yourself through the cards. With these cards, I wanted to keep it simple while also keeping it fun, so fun that I even made a '1' card, fail haha.

Personalized Cards

Statement

Along with the playing cards, I decided to add a little bit more of my own twist and create my own personalized cards. I thought this concept was very cool because I even saw people printing theirs out and actually use them physically and I thought that was pretty cool. My cards are reflecting the art deco era. It is mainly influenced by Noël Fontanet. Noël was born in Geneva and worked as a caricaturist and graphic designer. Fontanet drew caricaturists for newspapers and also designed political posters. Fontanet is someone who really thought outside of the box and that is always a number 1 thing for me. For my colors, I chose green and black which were derived from my personal branding that I've been working on previously (several iterations ago). I used a font called "Adamas Regular" which gives a geometric shape feel. Also, for the back of my cards I used a thick border to better bring that blue and black feel. The spades on the front of the card also have a geometric feel to bring it all together from the front to the back.
