Scenario / Challenge:

251 babies are born worldwide per minute. Did you catch that? Two hundred and fifty one! That's 502 parents that will have (hopefully) already chosen a name, but how? Well, they may tell you that they read a book, an article, or simply chosen a name already in the family. Whatever the case may be, these parents will tell you that they had a difficult time choosing the right fit, because afterall, a name is forever, right? But, no worries! Unforgettable names is here to help!

Solution:

Unforgettable Names allows you to get hundreds of names just by choosing a gender. With the random generator, you can get names all day long based on a 'male' or 'female' preference.
