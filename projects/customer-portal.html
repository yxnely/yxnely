Description:

A portal used by customers to help view their homeowners insurance information all in one place. Includes the ability to file a claim, make a payment, and view documents.

About the Project

Worked with business analysts, developers, and project manager to enhance the customer portal by adhering to the brand guidelines. Created wireframes, prototypes, and helped write business requirements to convey functionality, motion interactions, and must-haves for the user.


Along with working on the UI & UX of the portal, I also helped with crafting the all-employee email to raise awareness and the customer email communication which introduced customers to the new portal. Making sure that employees are trained on the new portal can ensure that customers will get help when calling in to our customer support team.
