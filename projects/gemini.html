Summary:

Scenario / Challenge:

Application to help warehouse workers keep track of packages that are received.

Solution:

A management system to track packages in states of 'pending' or 'completed'. Warehouse workers are able to check off and leave comments on the package in question. It will keep a history and each change will be logged with that particular worker.

Technologies Used:

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, MarvelApp
