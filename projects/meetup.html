Intro

A web and mobile app that will let users be able to host their own car meet-up or find some of their own to attend. Users can mark that they will attend, as well as share events to friends so that they too can attend.

Intended Audience

This app would be for car enthusiast that rebuild their car or hope to in the future and would like to check out other similar cars.
